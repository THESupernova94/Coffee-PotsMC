# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps create a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and kind
- Welcoming different opinions and experiences
- Giving constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility for mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insults, or offensive comments
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing someone’s private information without permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by filling out the [**Reporting Violations Google Form**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScvcxWcZQWzNTTGfIGHv4apGnssBxsbaByJzHu-YQWteaOFdw/viewform?usp=publish-editor).

Please note that reports may take 24-72 hours to be looked into, please be patient.

All reports will be handled respectfully and as privately as possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will review all reports and take action depending on the severity of the violation and the number of previous violations.

Consequences follow this ladder: 1-3 Warnings -> 1-2 Temporary Bans -> Permanent Ban.
